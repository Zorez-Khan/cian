Overview
Introduction

We are pleased to submit this Quotation cum Proposal to Cian Enterprises for providing a complete Manpower Solution at the TSUISL-TMH site. Our engagement covers end-to-end labour supply, job execution, quality oversight, and material handling — ensuring seamless site operations with minimal administrative burden on your team.

All rates quoted herein are exclusive of GST and are valid for 30 days from the date of this proposal.

We carry out Civil, Mechanical, and Housekeeping Manpower Solutions and look forward to working with your esteemed organisation on this and future assignments. Thank you for the opportunity.

07
Liabilities
Client Obligations & Exclusions
🏛️
Statutory Labour Benefits
All statutory benefits applicable to deployed labours — including Provident Fund (PF), ESIC, and any other government-mandated labour welfare contributions — shall be borne entirely by Cian Enterprises.
🧱
Material Cost
All costs related to procurement, supply, and replenishment of materials required at the worksite shall be borne by Cian Enterprises. ZK Ventures' scope covers handling and minimising spoilage only.
🚛
Transport Expense
Transportation of labours, materials, tools and tackles to and from the worksite shall be arranged and paid for by Cian Enterprises. Explicitly excluded from the quoted rates.
🔒
Theft Control
Responsibility for material theft control and security arrangements at the worksite rests with Cian Enterprises. ZK Ventures bears no liability for material losses due to theft.
🦺
Tools, Tackles & Safety Equipment
All tools, tackles, safety harness, and related personal protective equipment — along with any associated training costs — shall not be borne by ZK Ventures and remain the responsibility of Cian Enterprises as the client.
⚖️
The quoted rates cover manpower management, supervision, QC, and coordination only. All costs of materials, statutory labour obligations (PF, ESIC, etc.), transport, site security, tools & tackles, safety equipment, and training are the sole responsibility of Cian Enterprises as the client.
